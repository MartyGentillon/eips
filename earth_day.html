We set up an exhibit to show how to take photographs. The light box was the way to show still life photography to the public.
The slide show of member's photographs gave a sample of the kinds of photography club members take with their cameras.
EIPS members were available to answer questions on photography and our club.
Kids were able to photograph still life's of their own design.
