We judge images the send meeting on third Thursday of the month with a subject. The interpretation or judgement of the photographer as the the meaning of the subject is open to broad imagination.

Subjects over the years have been topics and other months have been photographer's choice.

Prior to November 2007 the images were either slides or 8 x 10 prints.

After November 2007 the images were digit images shown here. We had no slides but did have prints.

Prints are not shown in this listing.

One of the missing months is January when our Advancement awards replaced showing images.

Other missing months are due to weather, dates of holidays, or other events conflicting.
