GPS Location Finders

Photographers, unlike hunters or fishermen, will tell the exact location where a photograph was taken. Other photographers can take a similar image, but the time of day, clouds, season, and many other things may have changed, so the second photographer's image most likely not be the same as the first.

The following links will help you find a location.
