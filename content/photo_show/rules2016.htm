EASTERN IDAHO PHOTOGRAPHIC SOCIETY 27th JURIED SHOW AND SALE
for amateurs and professionals at EASTERN IDAHO TECHNICAL COLLEGE GALLERY
1600 South 2500 East, Idaho Falls, Idaho, October 8, 2016 – November 18, 2016
Rules and Regulations of the Show

1. Each applicant may enter up to five prints.

2. Judging is by 3 judges from the community. They award points to images. The points are totaled and prints for Best of Show and other placing awards are selected by points and judges' preference.

3. Best of Show (18%), first place (16%), second place (14%), third place (12%), and fourth place (10%) will return 70 % of all entry fees. In addition, blue ribbons will be awarded by the judges to the best "Animals", "Botanicals", "Landscape/Scenic", and "Creative" prints. Honorable Mention ribbons will also be awarded in each of these categories. (Best of Show through fourth winners are not eligible for the ribbons.) Prizes will be awarded to other entrants based on points and number of prizes available.

4. All prints must be at least 8 x 10 inches (before cropping) but no larger than 16 x 24 inches.

5. All prints must be FRAMED with WIRE HANGING DEVICES in place. Prints unable to be hung WILL NOT BE JUDGED AND THE ENTRY FEE WILL NOT BE RETURNED!!!

6. All prints must have the photographer’s name, address, phone number, PRINT TITLE, and price on the back of the finished framed print in the upper left hand corner. No name will be allowed on the front of the picture during the judging. Your business card may be placed on the print AFTER THE PRINT IS HUNG.

Note: it is important to have name, title, and other information on the back of each print. The information is needed to judge images, award ribbons, and attach name tags.

7. All prints may be for sale, with 5% retained by the Eastern Idaho Photographic Society.

8. All prints must be in EITC Room 371, 1600 S 2500 East, Idaho Falls, Idaho, by 6 p.m. on Saturday, October 8, 2016. Prints can be delivered on October 7 between 5 and 9 pm and on October 8 between noon and 6 pm. Judging will start at 7 pm on October 8. Print pickup is on November 18 before 5:00 p.m.

9. The Eastern Idaho Technical College and EIPS reserve the right not to hang all entries but will endeavor to hang the best 100 - 150 images depending on space availability.

10. The Eastern Idaho Technical College has no information on the show. ALL QUESTION ON THE SHOW MUST BE DIRECTED TO THE EASTERN IDAHO PHOTOGRAPHIC SOCIETY, 208-881-7944 or eips@eips.net.
