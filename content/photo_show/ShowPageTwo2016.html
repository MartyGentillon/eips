October Juried Show

Eastern Idaho Photographic Society has for over 25 years had a Juried Show in October. The purpose of the show is to have our photographs
judged and then displayed for the public to view. This is an educational and enjoyable experience for club members.
We encourage club members and others to enter their photographs in the show.

Three professional photographers, instructors of photography, or individuals working in the photography industry in the community or nearby areas
are selected to judge the show. The Best of Show, First Place, Second Place, Third Place, and Fourth Place photographs are selected.
In addition, a blue ribbon photograph is selected from each of the (optional) show categories.
If there are enough entries, an honorable mention is awarded in each show category.
All entries are ranked by scores given by the judges and prizes are awarded.

Photographs are hung for display for the general public to view.
